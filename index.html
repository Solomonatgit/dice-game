About The Game
Welcome to "Let's Shake" info center

The objective of Let's Shake is to have the highest score at the end of the round to be declared a winner.

Game Rules:

Player 1 and player 2 have to roll the dice 10 times before the the round winner is announced

when the players throw matching numbers it's a draw and the player that throws a bigger number wins the throw. on each roll the players score is added to the next throw and the player who has the highest score after rolling ten times is declared the winner.

Enjoy!
